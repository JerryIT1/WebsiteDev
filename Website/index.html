Welcome to FRACKT.
Join the NFT fractal community

NFT projects so far have primarily been images generated from dozens of photo layers, that are combined to generate thousands of NFTs. By using the beauty of math to generate fractals, FRACKT steers away from this formula to introduce a new, more beautiful NFT. This project brings a whole new meaning to unique NFTs by using formulas to create many complex 3D fractals that are completely different from the rest of the FRACKT collection. Check out the gallery page to see how crazy the variation is.
